## Description
PetPal is a social network designed to connect pet owners with pet service providers. Dog walkers, pet sitters, and other pet service providers can use our app to advertise their services to potential customers. At the same time, people who are looking for pet services can browse our app to discover pet service providers in their local community.

## App Functions

customer stories are implemented end to end with FreeMarker pages, Spring MVC controllers, services, repositories, and PostgreSQL persistence.

1. **US-1 — Customer Account and Profile Management:** A customer can sign up, log in, log out, update their contact information and profile image, and delete their account.
2. **US-2 — Pet Profile Management:** A customer can create, update, and delete pet profiles containing a name, species or breed, age, image, traits, and special-care instructions.
3. **US-3 — Browse and Filter Providers:** A customer can browse the provider directory and filter providers by keyword, location, service, and maximum rate.
4. **US-4 — View and Contact a Provider:** A customer can view a provider's description, services, rates, reviews, phone number, and email address and contact the provider outside PetPal.
5. **US-5 — Leave and Manage a Review:** A customer can recommend or not recommend a provider, leave a written review, view the provider's single response, and delete their own review.



2. Provider:
    1. **Create/modify/remove publically viewable profile which includes contact info.**
    2. Create a list of pet services with included location, prices, and times available.
    3. Post updates about pet services which can include photos and videos.
    4. **Reply to customer reviews.**
