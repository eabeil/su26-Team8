## Description
PetPal is a social network designed to connect pet owners with pet service providers. Dog walkers, pet sitters, and other pet service providers can use our app to advertise their services to potential customers. At the same time, people who are looking for pet services can browse our app to discover pet service providers in their local community.

## App Functions

1. Customer:
    1. Customer Account and Profile Management: A customer can sign up, log in, log out, update their contact information and profile image, and delete their account.
    2. Pet Profile Management:A customer can create, update, and delete pet profiles containing a name, species or breed, age, image, traits, and special-care instructions.
    3. Browse and Filter Providers: A customer can browse the provider directory and filter providers by keyword, location, service, and maximum rate.
    4. Leave and Manage a Review: A customer can recommend or not recommend a provider, leave a written review, view the provider's single response, and delete their own review.



2. Provider:
    **US-20 — Provider Account Creation:** As a provider, I want to create a profile so that I can use PetPal.
    **US-21 — Provider Pet Services** As a provider, I want to create a list of services so I can advertise what I offer.
    **US-22 — Provider Updates:** As a provider, I want to create updates so that customers can see what I am doing.
    **US-23 — Provider Review Responses:** As a provider, I want to respond to customer reviews so that I can address their concerns.
